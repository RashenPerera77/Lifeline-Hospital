24hrs Ambulance Service(Hotline 1616)

Our hospital offers a 24-hour ambulance service to provide prompt medical assistance in emergencies(Hotline 1616). Our fleet of well-equipped ambulances is staffed by trained medical professionals who are ready to respond to calls at any time. Whether it's a sudden illness, accident, or other medical emergency, our ambulance service is committed to transporting patients safely and efficiently to our hospital or other appropriate healthcare facilities.
